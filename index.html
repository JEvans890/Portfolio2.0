About Me

Hello! First of all, thank you for visiting my page! My name nickname is Jack, and I was born and raised inside of Las Vegas, NV where I stayed for 23 years. Some of My education includes:

  • Graduating Highschool at Shadow Ridge
  • Obtaining my Basic and Advanced certifications as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) from EMS Training Center
  • Some college credits towards an Associates degree in Paramedicine at the College of Southern Nevada (CSN)
  • Certifications as a Firefighter 1 and Hazardous Materials Awareness and Operations
Before Moving to the Portland, OR area, I had the opportunity to pursue my passion for emergency responding through my work experience at Medicwest Ambulance, Community Ambulance, Las Vegas Fire & Rescue, and Clark County Fire Department.

I always had an inclination towards entrepreneurship, but being so enveloped in studying and physical fitness to be the best at my job, I had not figured out exactly what I wanted to pursue in that regard. After deciding to leave, I found a passion for mushrooms and decided to go full-force with the work ethic I had obtained from my previous experiences to create my company, MushroomMedic! By trying to achieve this goal, I have continued to educate myself in mushroom cultivation and am currently pursuing a certification in Web Development at Epicodus.
